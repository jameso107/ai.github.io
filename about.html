About James

James Oosterhouse is a robotics and AI strategist currently studying Robotics at the University of Michigan’s College of Engineering, paired with a minor in Coaching & Leadership. With a rich track record leading FIRST Robotics Team 107 through multiple state- and world-level championships, James brings technical prowess, strategic thinking, and proven leadership to every business challenge.

As founding Director of Finance at Michigan Engineering Consulting Group and through industry internships at Koops Automation Systems, James bridges deep engineering insight with real‑world AI and automation applications.

Known for a philosophy rooted in human‑centered design and ethical innovation, James delivers customized, high-impact AI adoption strategies that empower businesses to thrive.
