At Koops Automation Systems, The House of AI led the design and deployment of internal AI tools aimed at accelerating and simplifying complex business development tasks. We focused on automating high-impact, repeatable workflows to boost efficiency across departments.

  • Machine Quoting Automation: Developed tools to analyze RFQs and generate machine concept estimates faster and more accurately, reducing quoting cycle time.
  • Sales Follow-Up Sequences: Automated intelligent reminders and dynamic email generation to ensure high engagement across the sales funnel.
  • Contract & Legal Review: Used natural language models to pre-screen contracts and highlight key risk clauses for legal and sales teams.
  • Task Intelligence Layer: Added prompt-driven decision support tools that help team members complete strategic tasks with minimal handoff or context switching.
